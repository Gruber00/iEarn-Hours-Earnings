# iEarn – Hours & Earnings

Track your work. Calculate your earnings. Reach your goals.

iEarn is a modern work hour and earnings tracker built exclusively for iPhone using SwiftUI and SwiftData. It helps you log your working hours, calculate your earnings automatically, and visualize your progress with beautiful interactive charts—all while keeping your data completely private on your device.

## Features

- ⏱️ Track work sessions with start time, end time, and optional breaks
- 💰 Automatic earnings calculation based on your hourly rate
- 📈 Monthly overview of earnings and working hours
- 📊 Interactive statistics with bar and line charts
- 🥧 Monthly goal tracking with an interactive progress chart
- 🎯 Custom monthly earnings goals
- 🏆 Achievement & milestone system with unlockable trophies
- 🕒 Optional smart 30-minute time rounding
- 🌍 Multiple languages (English, German, French & Spanish)
- 💱 Multiple currencies
- 🎨 Customizable accent colors
- 👈 Left- and right-handed layouts
- 📱 Beautiful onboarding experience
- ✨ Native iOS animations including success confirmation and confetti
- ⚡ Built entirely with SwiftUI & SwiftData
- 🔒 Offline-first and privacy-focused
- 🚫 No subscriptions
- 💳 One-time purchase
- 📡 No account required
- 📢 No advertisements
- 🛡️ No tracking or analytics

## Requirements

- iOS 26.0 or later
- Xcode 26 or later
- SwiftUI
- SwiftData

## Privacy

iEarn stores all work entries, settings, statistics, goals, and achievements locally on your device using SwiftData.

Your data never leaves your device.

- No user accounts
- No cloud dependency
- No advertisements
- No analytics
- No tracking
- 100% offline
