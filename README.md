# Work Hours

Track your work. Calculate your earnings. Reach your goals.

Work Hours is a modern work time tracker built exclusively for iPhone using SwiftUI and SwiftData. It helps you log your working hours, calculate earnings automatically and visualize your progress with beautiful interactive charts.

## Features

- ⏱️ Track working hours with start and end times
- 💰 Automatic earnings calculation
- 📊 Interactive statistics with bar and line charts
- 🎯 Monthly earnings goals
- 🏆 Achievement & milestone system
- 🕒 Optional smart time rounding
- 🌍 Multiple languages
- 💱 Multiple currencies
- 🎨 Custom accent colors
- 👈 Left & right-handed layouts
- 📱 Beautiful onboarding experience
- ⚡ Built with SwiftUI & SwiftData
- 🔒 Offline-first & privacy-friendly
- 🚫 No subscriptions
- 💳 One-time purchase

## Requirements

- iOS 26.0 or later
- Xcode 26 or later

## Privacy

Work Hours stores your data locally on your device using SwiftData.

No accounts required.
No cloud required.
No ads.
No tracking.
