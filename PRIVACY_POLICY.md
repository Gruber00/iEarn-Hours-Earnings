# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 29, 2026

## Overview

Thank you for using **iEarn**.

Your privacy is important. iEarn has been designed with privacy as a core principle. The app operates entirely on your device and does not collect, transmit, or sell your personal information.

---

## Information We Collect

**iEarn does not collect any personal information.**

The app does not collect or process:

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Postal address
- Location data
- Contacts
- Photos
- Camera data
- Microphone recordings
- Health information
- Financial information
- Device identifiers
- Advertising identifiers
- Analytics data
- Crash reports
- Browsing history
- Usage statistics

---

## Data Storage

All information entered into iEarn is stored **locally on your device** using Apple's SwiftData framework.

This includes, but is not limited to:

- Work entries
- Start and end times
- Break durations
- Hourly rate
- Monthly goals
- Statistics
- Trophy progress
- Achievement timestamps
- Preferred language
- Currency
- Accent color
- Chart style
- Hand preference
- App preferences

Your data never leaves your device.

---

## Internet Connection

iEarn works completely offline.

An internet connection is not required for any feature of the app.

---

## User Accounts

iEarn does not require:

- User registration
- User accounts
- Login
- Passwords

---

## Cloud Services

iEarn does not use cloud storage.

Your information is never uploaded to external servers.

---

## Third-Party Services

iEarn does not integrate with third-party services such as:

- Google Analytics
- Firebase
- Advertising SDKs
- Social media SDKs
- Marketing platforms
- Tracking services
- External databases

---

## Advertising

iEarn contains **no advertisements**.

---

## Tracking

iEarn does **not** track your activity.

No analytics or advertising identifiers are collected.

---

## Data Sharing

Your data is **never shared** with third parties.

---

## Children's Privacy

iEarn does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## Data Deletion

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you remain in complete control.

Deleting the app removes all locally stored data unless it has been included in an encrypted device backup created by you.
